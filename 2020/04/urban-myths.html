Watch Free 🎦 Urban Myths

Genre: Thriller
Released: 2020
Director: Kim Marie
Starring: Lou Ferrigno Jr., Courtney Gains, Joe Estevez
A group of high school seniors embark on a spring break camping trip to investigate the myths they had been researching and learning from their History teacher.

During their trip, they stumble on an old haunted Indian Burial ground and get more than they bargained for when some of the group start to vanish. This film showcases some of the best that Northern Michigan has to offer, along with an all-star cast.
