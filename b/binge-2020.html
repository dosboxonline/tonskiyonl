Watch Free 🎦 The Binge

Genre: Comedy
Released: 2020
Director: Jeremy Garelick
Starring: Vince Vaughn, Skyler Gisondo, Dexter Darden
In the not-so-distant future, all drinking and drugs have been made completely illegal by the government, except for one night a year. High school seniors Griffin, Hags, and Andrew make a pilgrimage to get to the best party in town where all their dreams will come true.

They will have to avoid their crazed principal, violent siblings, and the wild animals roaming the streets.
